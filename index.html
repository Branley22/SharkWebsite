The Fearsome Predator of the Sea: The Great White

Fun facts about this Great White Shark!

  1. The Great White is at the top of the food chain with a few threats being other larger sharks, orcas, and humans
  2. While white sharks are young, they feed on smaller fish. As they grow, they start to feed more on larger mammals like small whales
  3. Many white sharks on average grow to 15th feet and some have been recoreded to grow up to 20 feet.
